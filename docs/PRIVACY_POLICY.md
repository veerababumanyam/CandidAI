# Privacy Policy for CandidAI

*Last Updated: July 1, 2023*

## Introduction

CandidAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using CandidAI, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

CandidAI may collect the following personal information:

- **API Keys**: If you choose to use your own API keys for OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google, these are stored locally in your browser's storage.
- **Audio Data**: When you enable audio capture, we process audio from your microphone to provide transcription services.
- **Transcription Content**: The text transcribed from your audio is processed to generate AI responses.
- **Interview History**: With your explicit consent, we may store your interview questions, answers, and performance metrics.
- **Calendar Events**: If you use the calendar feature, we store event details such as interview dates, companies, and positions.

### Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information, including:

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the extension, such as features accessed and settings preferences.
- **Performance Data**: Technical information about extension performance, such as response times and error rates.
- **Browser Information**: Basic information about your browser environment necessary for the extension to function.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the CandidAI extension
- Improve and personalize your experience
- Generate AI-powered interview responses
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- Store interview history (only with explicit consent)
- Manage calendar events and reminders
- Troubleshoot issues and optimize performance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs:

- API keys are stored in Chrome's local storage
- Interview history is stored in Chrome's local storage
- Calendar events are stored in Chrome's local storage
- Settings and preferences are stored in Chrome's local storage

### Third-Party Services

When you use CandidAI with external AI services, your prompts and queries are sent to those services according to their respective privacy policies:

- **OpenAI**: If you use OpenAI services, your prompts are processed according to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/).
- **Anthropic**: If you use Anthropic services, your prompts are processed according to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
- **Google**: If you use Google services, your prompts are processed according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of sensitive data in transit
- Secure storage of API keys
- Local-first data storage approach
- Minimal data collection principle
- Optional features requiring explicit consent

## User Consent

### Explicit Consent Requirements

CandidAI requires explicit user consent for:

1. **Microphone Access**: Required for audio capture and transcription
2. **Interview History Storage**: Optional feature that stores interview data
3. **Screen Capture**: Required for visual analysis features
4. **Notifications**: Required for calendar reminders

You can withdraw consent at any time through the extension's settings.

## Data Retention

- **API Keys**: Stored until you remove them or uninstall the extension
- **Interview History**: Stored until you delete it or disable history storage
- **Calendar Events**: Stored until you delete them or uninstall the extension
- **Settings**: Stored until you reset them or uninstall the extension

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

- The right to access your personal information
- The right to correct inaccurate information
- The right to delete your personal information
- The right to restrict or object to processing
- The right to data portability
- The right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please use the extension's settings or contact us directly.

## Children's Privacy

CandidAI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will delete that information promptly.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Compliance

### GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This includes:

- Processing data lawfully, fairly, and transparently
- Collecting data for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes
- Minimizing data collection to what is necessary
- Ensuring data accuracy
- Limiting storage periods
- Ensuring appropriate security measures

### CCPA Compliance

For California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if your personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal information
- Access your personal information
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

## Cookie Policy

CandidAI does not use cookies directly. However, the third-party services we integrate with may use cookies according to their respective cookie policies.
